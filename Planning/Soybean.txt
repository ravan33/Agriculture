Soybean
•	Planting Time: June to July (Kharif season).
•	Soil: Well-drained loamy soil.
•	Irrigation: Rainfed, but supplemental irrigation during dry spells helps improve yield.
•	Fertilization: Balanced application of NPK; Rhizobium seed treatment to improve nitrogen fixation.
•	Harvesting: October to November.
•	Yield Improvement Tips:
o	Ensure timely sowing.
o	Monitor for diseases like rust and pests such as pod borers.
•	Soybean Farming Steps:
o	Step 1: Seed Selection: Choose high-yielding, disease-resistant varieties.
o	Step 2: Land Preparation: Deep plowing followed by harrowing.
o	Step 3: Sowing: Sow seeds 5 cm deep with 45 cm row spacing.
o	Step 4: Irrigation: Soybean is mainly rainfed, but supplementary irrigation is needed in dry spells.
o	Step 5: Fertilization: Apply phosphorus-rich fertilizers and use Rhizobium seed inoculation.
o	Step 6: Pest Management: Control diseases like rust and pests like stem borers.
o	Step 7: Harvesting: Harvest when 90% of pods turn yellow and leaves start dropping.
