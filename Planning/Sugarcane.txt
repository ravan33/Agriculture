Sugarcane
•	Planting Time: December to February (for early planting) or June to July (monsoon planting).
•	Soil: Loamy soil with good drainage.
•	Irrigation: Regular irrigation required throughout the crop cycle (depends on region and season).
•	Fertilization: High nitrogen and potassium requirements. Apply organic manure and NPK.
•	Harvesting: After 10-14 months, from January to March.
•	Yield Improvement Tips:
•	Use high-yielding varieties.
•	Regular weeding and application of biofertilizers.
•	Sugarcane Farming Steps:
o	Step 1: Setts Selection: Use disease-free setts with at least 2-3 buds.
o	Step 2: Land Preparation: Deep plowing followed by leveling.
o	Step 3: Planting: Plant setts horizontally in furrows with 1.5-meter row spacing.
o	Step 4: Irrigation: Provide regular irrigation, especially during critical growth stages.
o	Step 5: Fertilization: Apply nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium fertilizers in splits.
o	Step 6: Weed Control: Regularly remove weeds, especially in the first 90-120 days, using manual weeding or herbicides to enhance sugarcane growth.
