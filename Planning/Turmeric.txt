Turmeric
•	Planting Time: May to June (at the onset of monsoon).
•	Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter.
•	Irrigation: Requires regular irrigation for optimum growth.
•	Fertilization: Organic manure is essential; apply nitrogen and phosphorus fertilizers.
•	Harvesting: After 7-9 months, from February to April.
•	Yield Improvement Tips:
o	Use disease-free seed rhizomes.
o	Regular weeding and soil mulching to retain moisture.
•	Turmeric Farming Steps:
o	Step 1: Rhizome Selection: Use disease-free rhizomes for planting.
o	Step 2: Land Preparation: Deep plowing and application of organic manure.
o	Step 3: Planting: Plant rhizomes in ridges and furrows with 30 cm row spacing.
o	Step 4: Irrigation: Regular irrigation is necessary, especially during dry spells.
o	Step 5: Fertilization: Use nitrogen and phosphorus fertilizers and apply FYM.
o	Step 6: Pest Management: Control rhizome rot and leaf spots.
o	Step 7: Harvesting: After 7-9 months, harvest when leaves turn yellow.
